A favorite hangout of the Beat Generation and surfers in search of big breaks, Mazatlan offers excellent beaches and a laid-back vibe. Mazatlan tours are the best way to experience the city—hop on an ATV or zipline in the morning, chow down on some famous Sinaloa seafood for lunch, and go on a kayak or e-bike ride in the afternoon. Or if that sounds too active, there’s always the option to book a beach getaway that takes the guesswork out of finding some of the area’s most secluded strips of sand.
