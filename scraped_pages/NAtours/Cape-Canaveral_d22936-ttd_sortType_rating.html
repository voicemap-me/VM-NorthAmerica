Cape Canaveral on Florida’s Space Coast may be best-known as a rocket launch site, but there are loads of other things to do in the area. Fun Cape Canaveral tours range from kayaking in mangrove forests to ocean fishing charters and lagoon paddleboarding excursions. On these water-based adventures, keep your eyes peeled for the dolphins and manatees that make their home here. Another entertaining option: a trip to the Wizard of Oz Museum with its impressive array of memorabilia and colorful photo ops.
