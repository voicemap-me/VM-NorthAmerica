Most of Alaska is wilderness, so Seward is a convenient base for exploring the great outdoors. The city is next to Kenai Fjords National Park and many Seward tours include excursions in the park. Cruises offer views of stunning glaciers and you might even spot wildlife such as otters, whales, porpoises, and puffins. If youd prefer something more adventurous, consider a guided glacier hike, a kayaking trip on Resurrection Bay, or even a dog sled ride.
