Whether you're looking for a relaxed vacation by the ocean or are more into action-packed days and party-filled nights, Puerto Vallarta will surely have something in store for you. Puerto Vallarta excursions for active travelers abound, making it possible to devote your getaway to ATVing, parasailing, or flying above the trees with the help of a zipline. If youd rather do something a bit more chill, you can go tequila tasting at a local distillery, hang out at hot springs, take cooking or chocolate-making classes, or head out with a guide to taste the best tacos in town.

Q:What are the best tours in Puerto Vallarta?
A:

There are many highly rated tours in Puerto Vallarta. These are the best right now, according to travelers:

  • Downtown Puerto Vallarta Food Tour with Vallarta Food Tours
  • Extreme Zip Line Adventure
  • Puerto Vallarta Original Canopy Tour, Ziplining, Tequila and Speed Boat Ride
  • Mega Yacht All-Inclusive Yelapa Waterfall & Snorkeling Adventure
  • Food and Mixology Tour: Tequila, Tacos, and Mexican Cocktails
Q:Are guided tours in Puerto Vallarta worth it?
A:

Yes, most people find that guided tours in Puerto Vallarta are worth it. Some tours take you behind the scenes of the areas food culture, allowing you to sample dishes and tequila at multiple locations. Others help you tap into the areas adventurous side, with tours that combine many different activities in one day, from ziplining to sailing to snorkeling and more.

Q:How much do Puerto Vallarta tours cost?
A:

There are Puerto Vallarta tours in just about every budget. Most of the outdoor adventure-focused tours run about US$90-140. Food tours are a popular pick in Puerto Vallarta; these cost around US$60. And if you have a bigger budget, your group can book a private yacht for around US$3,000.

Q:What activities are included on Puerto Vallarta tours?
A:

Puerto Vallarta tours showcase the areas personality, with a combination of outdoor adventures and forays into the local food culture. You can pick from options like whale watching, sailing, snorkeling, and ATV tours, and then fill up during a cooking class or a food tour showcasing local restaurants.

Q:How many days in Puerto Vallarta is enough?
A:

Four to five days in Puerto Vallarta is generally enough to experience the highlights. This length of stay gives you time to explore the area at a relaxed pace, with time for ziplining, snorkeling, and sailing tours. Youll also have time to try several local restaurants and relax on the areas beautiful beaches.

Q:What is the best month to visit Puerto Vallarta?
A:

The best month to visit Puerto Vallarta is November. The weather is warm and pleasant, marking the end of the rainy season and the beginning of the dry season. This period offers fewer tourists compared to the peak winter months, allowing for a more relaxed experience and often lower prices.
