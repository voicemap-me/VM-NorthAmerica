On Isla Holbox, life slows downwith no bridge to the mainland, the pace here is a world away from the spring breakers of Cancun and the arty hedonism of Tulum. But if you get tired of lounging, Isla Holbox tours can supplement your vacation with some exploration. Visit bioluminescent waters, mangrove forests, and remote islands such as Isla de los Pjaros in a kayak. The nearby waters even count gargantuan whale sharks among their residents.
