Famous for its white sand beaches along Florida’s dazzling Emerald Coast, Destin is best explored from the water. Ride waves with dolphins on a jet ski, see their fins from above on a parasailing adventure, or follow their pods on a banana boat ride with friends. Snorkeling in Choctawhatchee Bay is a great way to spot colorful fish and turtles, while an excursion to Crab Island—a submerged sandbar favored by boaters—is perhaps the most iconic experience in Destin. Hang out on a boozy tiki cruise or enjoy the marine life from a kayak or paddleboard—Destin tours span all ages and activity preferences.
