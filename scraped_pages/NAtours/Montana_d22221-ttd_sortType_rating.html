What Montana lacks in people—it’s one of the least populated states despite its massive size—it more than makes up for with scenery. Montana tours are your ticket to Glacier National Park, Yellowstone National Park, and almost every mountain and river. Of course, expert guides can also show you the best of Big Sky Country’s mountain towns, where you’ll find pottery workshops, cooking classes, and more. And as long as you’re not allergic to snow, it’s a four-season destination where you don’t have to worry too much about crowds and traffic jams.
