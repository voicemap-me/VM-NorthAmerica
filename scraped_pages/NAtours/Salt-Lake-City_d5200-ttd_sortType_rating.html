Salt Lake City also makes a great base for exploring the region, with excursions to the Bonneville Salt Flats or Wasatch Mountains.

Private Half-Day Drive Through the Wasatch Mountain Range

*Start times are flexible* Travel through one of the most scenic mountain drives in the entire country. We will travel from Salt Lake through Parley's Canyon into the backside of the Wasatch mountain range (known as the Wasatch Back), passing historic Park City. From there we turn south and view beautiful Jordanelle Reservoir, before eventually reaching the mountain town of Heber. From Heber we turn West and view the incredible Deer Creek Reservoir in the shadow of Mount Timpanogos. We soon descend into Provo canyon, following a meandering river, beneath tall mountains and cliffs. Eventually we will see Bridal Veil falls, where travels (if they choose) can take the short walk to see up close. Our return to Salt Lake starts with a drive through one of the areas most beautiful drives; The Alpine loop! Travel past Sundance Ski Resort (Founded by Robert Redford) through switch backs, up and over a mountain and back down the other side! Watch for wild mountain Goats along the way!
