North Carolina tours could have you paddling through white water on the French Broad, searching for wild horses on an off-road adventure, or cruising the Blue Ridge Parkway with a naturalist guide. If youre staying near a city such as Charlotte or Wilmington, consider tours that introduce you to local culture, including history tours, pub crawls, and ghost tours.
