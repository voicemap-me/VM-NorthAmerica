Santa Fe feels like a breath of fresh air in many waysand thats not just a figure of speech. Santa Fe tours take nature lovers on hikes in the surrounding hills or e-bike tours through downtown, stopping at hidden gems like the Santa Fe Railyard and River Park. You can also follow local guides on walking tours through the historic Plaza, passing landmarks like the Loretto Chapel and Barrio de Analco. For foodies and architecture buffs, curated excursions highlight the citys dynamic culinary scene and centuries-old adobe buildings that define Santa Fes distinctive aesthetic.
