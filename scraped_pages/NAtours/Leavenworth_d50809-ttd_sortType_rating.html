Year-Round Private Columbia River Boat Cruise in Wenatchee, WA

Just a short drive from Leavenworth, WA, immerse yourself in the natural beauty of the iconic Columbia River with a private boat cruise in the heart of Washington state. Leave the stress of boating behind and relax as a professional Captain navigates one of Chelan County’s most majestic waterways. Your journey begins with a scenic cruise northward under the US 2 Bridge, leading to the impressive Rocky Reach Dam. Here, the captain will turn off the engine, offering fascinating insights about the dam and its surrounding landscape.
