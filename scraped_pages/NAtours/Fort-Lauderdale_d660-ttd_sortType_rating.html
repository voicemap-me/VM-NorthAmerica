Fort Lauderdale is synonymous with superyachts and opulent waterfront mansions, which are best seen by boat. Hop on a cruise with pirate games or dinner and show, then sit back and take in the city’s coastal landmarks. If you’d prefer to get away from it all, other Fort Lauderdale tours highlight nearby nature. Try fishing, snorkeling, or parasailing, or take a winding airboat ride through the grassy Everglades to spot alligators and manatees with a seasoned naturalist.
