Situated on Florida’s Gulf Coast, this destination is a haven for beachgoers. And though you might have the beach part covered, you can make your getaway even more spectacular by adding one of these Fort Myers tours to your itinerary. Options range from kayaking trips to sunset dolphin-watching cruises, getting you out on the water and exploring the best of this beautiful place. You can even try shelling cruises, aimed at helping you find shell treasures in the best spots that only your experienced boat captain knows where to look.
