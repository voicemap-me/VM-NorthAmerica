When you visit Sarasota, youd be forgiven for wanting to spend every spare moment lying on the beachbut then you would miss out on a ton of fun activities. There are Sarasota tours, for instance, that take you kayaking in areas known for manatees, parasailing over nearby islands, boating to look for dolphins, or fishing for trout. There are even nighttime boating adventures with clear-bottom kayaks so you can see all sorts of life underwater. Back on dry land, you can also take an expert-led walk to explore the citys architecture and cuisine.
