Known for its sugar-white sand and aqua-hued waters, this Northwest Florida beach town offers plenty of ways to relax, unwind, and have fun. Panama City Beach tours are led by local guides who will take you on an excursion to Shell Island to search for wild dolphins or on a three-hour adventure cruise where you can kayak, paddleboard, snorkel, and explore an inflatable waterpark or take a 2-hour sunset cruise. After a day at the beach, rinse off the sunscreen, grab your friends and go ax throwing, or try your luck at an escape room or haunted attraction in town.
