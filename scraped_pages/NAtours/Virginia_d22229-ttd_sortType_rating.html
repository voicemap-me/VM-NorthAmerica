History is everywhere in Virginia, so heritage is a common theme on Virginia tours. You can visit the Jamestown Settlement and Colonial Williamsburg, crucial battlefields of the American Revolution, the plantation homes of Presidents Washington and Jefferson, the White House of the Confederacy, and locations important to Virginia’s African-American history. Other activities include sampling local eats on a food-focused city walk, wine tasting in the Blue Ridge Mountains, and kayaking in the Chesapeake Bay.
