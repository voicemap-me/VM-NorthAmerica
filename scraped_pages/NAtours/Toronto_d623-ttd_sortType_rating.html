Multicultural Toronto is governed by its seasons: In the sub-zero winters, locals and visitors head underground and indoors to world-class museums and galleries. But come summer, Toronto thaws and the sun shines—the ideal weather for exploring on foot or bicycle, sailing or paddling on Lake Ontario, or snagging tickets to sporting events. Toronto’s proximity to Niagara Falls also makes it easy to check out the mighty rushes of water on the Canada–US border on a day trip. Admire the spectacle from a safe distance or have fun in the splash zone either up close on a boat ride or behind the falls.
