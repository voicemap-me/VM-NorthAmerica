Vancouver is both cosmopolitan and connected to nature, making it a great place to enjoy the city lights before heading out into the forest. Vancouver tours make it easy to see the highlights of the city by bus, get the chance to spot whales in the sea or visit beloved attractions such as the Capilano Suspension Bridge or sprawling Stanley Park. There are loads of options that will get you out of town, too, to popular nearby destinations such as BC's capital city, Victoria, and the celebrated ski resort of Whistler.

Q:What in Vancouver is worth seeing?
A:

Vancouver is a striking city of glass highrises set against a mountainous backdrop. There are plenty of worthwhile attractions here, including the lush forests of Stanley Park, the charming brick facades of the Gastown district, and the bustling Granville Island Market. Nearby, hiking trails and suspension bridges through the forest beckon adventurous travelers.

Q:How much do Vancouver tours cost?
A:

Whether youre on a tight budget or have more wiggle room, theres a Vancouver tour for every price range. You can join a walking tour for a few dollars per person, or shell out for a multi-day tour that brings your group out of the city and to the mountains and nearby islands, or even to Jasper National Park.

Q:Does Vancouver have hop-on hop-off tours?
A:

Yes, Vancouver has hop-on hop-off tours. These are a popular option among Vancouver visitors, as the buses stop at major city highlights like Chinatown, Stanley Park, and Gastown, and offer live commentary about the places youre passing along the way. Youll also have the benefit of flexibility, as these tours allow you to explore at your own pace.

Q:What activities can you do in Vancouver?
A:

Vancouver is packed with activities offering a variety of experiences. You might choose to see the citys highlights on a Vancouver sightseeing tour, cruise the citys waterways, or head out to nearby parks and mountains. Foodies will love the culinary tours, while adrenaline-seekers can choose a ziplining experience or kayaking tour.

Q:How many days in Vancouver is enough?
A:

The ideal duration for a visit to Vancouver depends on your interests and the activities you are planning. Most travelers find that spending around 35 days in Vancouver allows ample time to explore the city's main attractions, enjoy outdoor adventures, and experience its cultural offerings. A longer trip would also allow time for out-of-the city excursions, such as a visit to Victoria, a foray into the mountains, or a trip to nearby islands.
