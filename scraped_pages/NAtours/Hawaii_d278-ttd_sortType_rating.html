Q:What are the best tours in Hawaii?
A:

With thousands of options to choose from, it can be helpful to know which Hawaii tours rank highest among previous travelers. Here are some of the top-rated tours at the moment:

  • Grand Circle Island and Haleiwa 9 Hour Tour
  • Honolulu Xtreme Parasail
  • Big Island in a Day: Volcanoes Waterfalls Sightseeing and History
  • North Shore Circle Island Adventure Including Snorkeling with the Turtles
  • Salute to Pearl harbor Including USS Arizona
Q:Are guided tours in Hawaii worth it?
A:

Yes, guided Hawaii excursions and tours are absolutely worth it. A guides expertise comes in handy, whether youre looking for a deeper understanding of local history or the best snorkeling spots. Depending on the tour you choose, your guide may also be your instructor to get you up to speed if youre a diving beginner, for instance, or new to surfing.

Q:How much do Hawaii tours cost?
A:

The price range of Hawaii excursions is pretty big, from a low end of around US$20 (or less) per person for some guided walks all the way up to full-day private boat charters for epic snorkeling or sailing adventures that can be US$8,000 or more per group. Many of the top-rated Hawaii excursions are between US$50150 per person.

Q:What activities are included on Hawaii tours?
A:

Given that there are so many tours available throughout the islands, the list of activities you can do on a Hawaii excursion is long. Depending on the tour you choose, your itinerary may include water sports like snorkeling or parasailing, hiking adventures to see volcanoes or waterfalls, visits to historic sights like Pearl Harbor or historic Kona, and so much more.

Q:How many days in Hawaii is enough?
A:

Most travelers suggest devoting at least one week per island, since theres so much to do on each one. If youre only visiting one island, an 810 day trip gives you a chance to see the major sights at a more relaxed pace. An extended stay on one island also means youll have time for longer Hawaii excursions like day-long snorkeling adventures.

Q:What is the best month to visit Hawaii?
A:

This depends a bit on which island youre visiting. The spring (roughly AprilMay) and fall (roughly SeptemberNovember) shoulder seasons are among the best times to be on Oahu or Maui, when the weather is lovely but its less crowded and hotel rooms are a little less expensive. On the Big Island, that combination of nice weather and lower prices runs from about SeptemberNovember. If your itinerary centers on seasonal things like whale watching or prime surfing conditions, though, youll want to plan ahead since those kinds of events mean high season crowds and higher prices.
