Orlando is nearly synonymous with its theme parks, but the Florida city offers plenty of other opportunities for family fun beyond the rides. Many of the most popular Orlando tours take visitors to the Kennedy Space Center for a day of educational fun. Others focus on the great outdoors of Central Florida, offering experiences that range from glass-bottom kayaking adventures to excursions into the Everglades aboard airboats.
