Tucked into the upper right-hand corner of the continental United States, Maine offers a fantastic combination of historic sites and natural wonders to entertain all sorts of travelers. Acadia National Park is arguably the states top outdoor attraction, and there are plenty of Maine tours that focus on its rugged beauty. There are outdoor adventures outside the park, too, including hitting the water in a tall ship or a former lobster boat. And once youve worked up an appetite, check out the many food-focused walks in culinary cities like Portland or Bar Harbor.
